What is a Paper Wallet?

A paper wallet is a means of maintaining a Bitcoin public and private key pair on a physical piece of paper. This allows you to keep your Bitcoin account information offline on a physical object. The private and public key pair is generated using a secure random address generator. Paper wallets are a great way to store Bitcoins, and if done properly can be very secure compared to other options.

Paper Wallet Features

Simple

Anyone can make a paper wallet! It's a great way to interact with the technology and have physical representation of your digital currency.

Fast

The wallet generation process only takes a minute. After that your paper wallet is ready to receive funds.

Secure

Our wallet generator is based on the empirically proven and globally trusted Bitaddress.org. An open source, client side Javascript wallet generator.

How it works

1

Use our random wallet generator to create a unique public and private key pair


2

Print out the wallet on a secure printer


3

Cut along the border and then fold the private key section over


4

Send any amount of Bitcoin Cash to the public address


5

Keep the wallet somewhere safe, or give it as a gift. When ready simply reveal the private key and scan it with a digital wallet to claim your funds
